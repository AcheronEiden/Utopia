Welcome, Chronicler

“Kill one man and you are a Murderer, kill a million and you are a Conqueror. Kill them all and you are a God.” — The Void Monarch

This manual serves as the nexus of the multiverse, a living document detailing the world of Utopia and the countless planes connected to it. Within lies the collected knowledge of its fundamental laws, the diverse races that inhabit it, the ethereal energy that permeates all existence, and the powerful entities who shape that destiny.

Your journey into the depths of this dark fantasy universe begins now.
