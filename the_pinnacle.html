Techniques & Arts

5.2 Pinnacle of Sorcery

Beyond the realm of spells lies a higher tier of magical prowess - Arts. These refined manipulations of mana are categorized by their complexity and power—ranging from Common Arts accessible to novices, to Legendary Arts known only to the most powerful sorcerers. While all arts elevate a sorcerer beyond simple spellcasting, it is the mastery of legendary arts that can transform them into true architects of reality.

5.2.1 Domain Declaration: Shaping Reality Itself

Domain Declaration is a legendary art that allows sorcerers to carve out a separate space within the fabric of reality. By using a vast amount of stored mana, typically equivalent to a Sixth Order Being's entire reserves, the sorcerer constructs a barrier and infuses it with their signature magical style. This creates a domain a self-contained battlefield where the sorcerer's abilities are significantly amplified.

Activating a domain requires a specific hand sign or personal signal, and from an outside perspective the domain appears as a floating black sphere. Within the domain, the caster's attacks become a sure-hit attack, unavoidable and undetectable. Their spells and abilities bend reality to ensure they strike their target, making them incredibly potent offensive tools.

However, this immense power comes at a steep cost. The sheer amount of mana required can be lethal, and the domain itself has a weak exterior barrier susceptible to external intervention. Additionally, a more refined domain can potentially overpower another, making domain battles tests of both power and tactical prowess.

5.2.2 The Eight-star Annihilation Art: Mastering Adaptation

The Eight-star Annihilation Art is a legendary art developed and solely used by the enigmatic Imaginary Demon, Sharon. This potent skill allows the user to adapt to any and all magical phenomena they encounter. Upon activation, the user triggers an eight-edged pentagram that initiates a rapid analysis of incoming attacks. With each subsequent attack of the same type, the analysis deepens.

Once the pentagram completes a full revolution, the user achieves complete adaptation, rendering them impervious to future assaults utilizing the same concept. This art is primarily considered a defensive maneuver, granting the user unparalleled resilience against repetitive attacks. However, some theorize the potential to use it against defensive arts as well, potentially allowing the user to bypass or exploit certain magical barriers.

5.3 A Compendium of Arts

While some arts are legendary and unique, many others are taught and practiced by sorcerers across the planes, categorized by their difficulty and power.

Common Arts

Mana Threading

A foundational art where a sorcerer extrudes their mana into fine, semi-solid threads. These threads can be used for delicate tasks, such as manipulating small objects from a distance, creating simple magical tripwires, or delivering a tiny jolt of energy. It is a fundamental skill for demonstrating precise mana control.

Aura Suppression

A basic mental discipline allowing a sorcerer to consciously dampen their own mana signature. By concentrating, they can appear less powerful or even non-magical to mundane senses. This art is essential for any sorcerer wishing to practice stealth, though it is easily pierced by specialized detection spells or truly powerful beings.

Mana Sense

A fundamental art where a sorcerer actively expands their awareness to feel the flow and concentration of mana in their immediate surroundings. This allows them to detect the presence of other living beings, hidden magical traps, or enchanted items. It is a foundational skill for scouting and awareness.

Focus Casting

A mental discipline used to accelerate the casting of lower-tier spells. By entering a state of intense concentration, a sorcerer can streamline their mana flow and shorten incantations, allowing for more rapid and efficient spellcasting in combat. This art is less effective on complex, high-tier spells that require their full activation methods.

Uncommon Arts

Elemental Infusion

A combat art where a sorcerer channels elemental mana and temporarily binds it to a mundane weapon, such as a sword or arrowhead. The weapon takes on the properties of that element—becoming wreathed in flame, coated in frost, or crackling with lightning. This is a staple art for close-combat sorcerers who blend physical combat with spellcasting.

Mana Burst

A close-range, defensive art where the user releases a sudden, omnidirectional blast of raw, untyped mana from their body. It lacks the destructive power of a focused spell but is excellent for forcefully pushing back multiple enemies in close quarters, creating an opportunity to escape or counter-attack.

Kinetic Redirection

A defensive reflex art where a sorcerer uses a precise, localized burst of force—often using wind or gravity magic—to alter the trajectory of an incoming physical projectile. It is not a solid barrier, but a skillful "nudge" that requires perfect timing to deflect attacks like arrows or thrown daggers.

Rare Arts

Spatial Flash

Opens a crack in the immediate space and lets its user travel through the void. Should only be used by those that can shield themselves from the void. Preferably by those that wield void magic.

Spell Splicing

A highly advanced and dangerous art where a sorcerer attempts to cast two different spells simultaneously, weaving their activation incantations and mana flows together. If successful, this can create a single, combined effect of immense power and unpredictability. However, failure often results in the spells violently backfiring on the caster.

Aspect of the Elements

The user can temporarily embody the pure essence of an element, altering her form and abilities. For example, taking on an Aspect of Stone to become nearly indestructible, or an Aspect of the Lightning to move as lightning.

Blood Pact Alchemy

A forbidden alchemical art, rumored to be practiced by high-ranking vampires of the Blood Empire. The alchemist uses their own life force (blood) as a potent catalyst to drastically accelerate potion synthesis or to create powerful, temporary homunculi loyal to their creator. The art offers incredible results at the personal cost of the user's own vitality.

Mana Imprint

An art used by master enchanters and dwarven artificers to "imprint" a small piece of their unique mana signature onto a crafted item. This creates a sympathetic link, allowing the creator to feel the general location and condition of the item from a great distance, or even activate a simple, pre-set magical effect within it.

Life Thread Severance

A forbidden assassination art related to the True Law of Death. The user must acquire a piece of the target's biological matter (like hair or blood) and perform a complex ritual to perceive their "life thread" in the Ethereal Plane. The user then attempts to sever this thread with a blade of pure mana. The art is incredibly risky; a target with a strong will can resist it, and failure can cause a severe backlash that damages the caster's own soul.

Hollow Sphere

A rare and highly specialized defensive art developed as a direct counter to a Domain Declaration. Instead of projecting a complex inner world, the user erects a small, personal barrier of "unwritten" reality around themselves. This sphere does not have its own rules; its sole purpose is to nullify the unavoidable hit effect of any domain it is cast within. Inside the Hollow Sphere, the absolute authority of the surrounding domain is temporarily negated, giving the user a small sanctuary where they are not automatically struck by the domain's infused attacks. While it offers crucial defense, the art is incredibly taxing on the user's concentration and can be shattered by a direct, powerful attack, making it a desperate but vital tool for survival.

Legendary Arts

Infernal Decree

Allows the user to issue a single, binding command to any being of a lower rank in their immediate vicinity. Those without sufficient willpower are magically compelled to obey the command to the best of their ability.

Domain Declaration
Reality Marble

A conceptual art on par with Domain Declaration. Instead of creating a field that guarantees attacks will hit, the user projects their own inner world and soul outwards, overwriting a small area of reality with their own set of physical and magical laws. This does not directly harm an opponent, but it forces them to fight on the user's conceptual "home turf," where the user holds the ultimate advantage and can dictate the rules of the battle.

Decree of Heaven

A legendary and forbidden art that allows the user to directly interfere with the threads of causality and fate. By expending a vast amount of their personal karmic luck and life force, the user issues a single, absolute, conditional statement—a "Decree"—that Heaven and Earth is compelled to enforce. This is not a prediction of the future, but a forced alteration of it. For example, a user could declare, 'The next spell my opponent casts will turn against them.' If the conditions are met, reality itself will conspire to make it so. The cost is astronomical; the user is left spiritually drained and plagued by terrible misfortune as their karmic luck replenishes. Furthermore, issuing a Decree that defies the Will of the Cosmos can cause a catastrophic backlash, potentially erasing the user from existence entirely.

5.4 The Martial Artist's Path: Techniques

Where Sorcerers learn Arts to weave mana, Martial Artists master Techniques to channel it. Techniques are the application of mana through the physical body, honed through relentless discipline and repetition. They are not cast like spells but are executed as an extension of a physical action, making them direct and reliable in the heat of battle.

Common Techniques

Swordsman Technique: Guard Break

A fundamental application of mana in swordsmanship. The user channels a sharp, concussive burst of mana into their blade at the moment of impact. This technique is not for cutting, but for shattering an opponent's guard, breaking simple shields, or forcing an opening in their defense.

Uncommon Techniques

Aura Blade

A signature technique for armed combatants. The user extends their mana from the edge of their weapon, creating a longer, sharper, non-physical cutting edge. This allows them to strike from a greater distance and slice through mundane armor with ease. Different martial schools have their own unique variations of this technique.

Impact Wave

The user channels a significant amount of mana into a physical strike (a punch, kick, or shield bash) and releases it in a concussive burst upon impact. This creates a short-range shockwave that can stagger or knock down nearby enemies who are not the primary target.

Bowman Technique: Mana Tracer Arrow

The user fires a specially crafted, non-lethal arrow infused with their own mana signature. If the arrow successfully strikes a target, it creates a "Mana Imprint" that links to the bowman's senses. This allows the bowman to feel the target's general location for a limited time, even through walls or natural cover.

Unarmed Fighter Technique: Adamant Palm

A refinement of the basic Reinforce technique. The user focuses their mana into their hands and feet, making them as hard and durable as steel. This allows the user to block bladed weapons without injury and deliver devastating, armor-breaking blows with their bare fists.

Rare Techniques

Iron Fortress Stance

A master-level defensive technique. The user adopts a specific, rooted stance and circulates a massive amount of mana throughout their body in a continuous, reinforcing loop. While in this stance, the user gains immense resilience to physical blows and becomes nearly immovable, but it rapidly drains stamina and leaves them unable to perform complex dodges.

Unarmed Fighter Technique: Hundred Fists Mirage

An advanced offensive technique in which the user augments their movements with explosive bursts of mana, allowing them to strike with such speed that they create tangible afterimages. This overwhelms an opponent's defenses with a barrage of blows that seem to come from multiple directions at once.

Swordsman Technique: Flowing Steel

An advanced technique where the swordsman channels mana not just into their blade, but through their entire body in a continuous loop. This grants them unnaturally fluid movements and preternatural reflexes, allowing them to deflect attacks with minimal effort and seamlessly redirect an opponent's momentum into a counter-attack.

Spearman Technique: Coiling Serpent

The user imbues their spear shaft with a large amount of flexible mana, allowing the normally rigid weapon to bend and whip like a serpent. This technique enables unpredictable, flexible thrusts that can strike around an opponent's shield or parry, making the spear's reach dangerously deceptive.

Legendary Techniques

Swordsman Technique: Blade of the Soul

A legendary technique that transcends physical combat. The user projects their own willpower and spirit through their sword, creating a blade of pure intent that can interact with the intangible. This allows the swordsman to cut through magical barriers, sever curses, and even harm spiritual or ethereal beings that have no physical form.

Spearman Technique: Gungnir's Judgment

The spearman hurls their weapon after imbuing it with the absolute concept of an attack that cannot miss. The spear relentlessly pursues its designated target, phasing through non-magical obstacles and bending its path to ensure it strikes true. Once the target is hit, the spear instantly vanishes and reappears in the user's hand.

Bowman Technique: Arrow of the Cosmos

The ultimate single-shot technique. The bowman draws their string without an arrow, using their own body as a conduit to gather ambient celestial energy—starlight, moonlight, or sunlight—into a single point of devastating power. The released projectile is a bolt of pure cosmic energy that travels at the speed of light and carries the properties of its source.

Unarmed Fighter Technique: Heaven-Shaking Stomp

The pinnacle of channeling mana through the body. The user focuses their entire and mana reserves into a single, devastating stomp. The resulting shockwave does not just crack the ground; it sends a resonance wave through the very ley lines of the earth, creating a localized, violent earthquake that can level structures and shatter armies.
