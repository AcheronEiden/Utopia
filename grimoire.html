Unveiling the Arsenal

Spells by Magic Type

Light Magic

  • Lesser Light (Tier 1): The most fundamental light spell, creating a small, floating orb of gentle light to illuminate the immediate area. A foundational spell for all aspirants.
  • Mend Minor Wounds (Tier 1): Channels a small amount of positive energy to close minor cuts and soothe bruises. It cannot treat serious injuries, diseases, or poisons.
  • Sacred Shield (Tier 5): Conjures a temporary, circular shield of solid light that can block low-tier physical and magical attacks. Requires focus to maintain.
  • Sunlance (Tier 10): Gathers ambient light into a spear-like projectile of searing radiant energy. It is highly effective against undead and shadow-based creatures.
  • Remove Curse (Tier 10): A complex ritual that uses pure light energy to unravel and dispel low-to-mid-tier curses from a person or object, purifying the target.
  • Speed of Light (Tier 15): Allows the caster to move at incredible speeds matching that of light
  • Consecrate Ground (Tier 15): Purifies a large area of land over several minutes, creating a zone of holy ground. Within this area, allies are slowly healed and protected from dark magic, while undead and demonic entities are severely weakened.
  • Maximize Magic: God's Arrival (Tier 20): A powerful spell that calls upon a divine entity from the Ethereal Plane to fight alongside the caster.
  • Maximize Magic: Purification (Tier 20): Targets the soul of the opponent, causing instant death to those weaker than the caster. This extremely potent spell requires significant mana and concentration to cast.

Dark Magic

  • Shadowmend (Tier 5): Uses threads of shadow energy to stitch wounds closed. While effective, the target feels a deathly chill until the magic fades.
  • Umbral Cloak (Tier 10): Wraps the caster in true, tangible darkness, rendering them undetectable to most forms of magical sight.
  • Life Drain (Tier 15): Siphons the life force from a target to replenish the caster's own vitality
  • Maximize Magic: God's Arrival (Tier 20): A powerful spell that calls upon a demonic entity from the Ethereal Plane to fight alongside the caster.

Necromancy Magic

  • Speak with the Dead (Tier 5): Allows the caster to briefly reanimate the vocal cords and memories of a corpse to ask a limited number of questions. A foundational, if unsettling, spell for investigators.
  • Animation (Tier 10): Reanimates a single humanoid corpse to serve as a mindless, shambling servant. The construct is slow and unintelligent, obeying only simple commands.
  • Soul Tap (Tier 15): A forbidden spell that allows the caster to forcibly extract a fragment of a soul from a recently deceased body to empower dark magic rituals or create magical items.
  • Maximize Magic: Lord of the Undying Legion (Tier 20): The caster channels immense negative energy into a large area, reanimating all corpses within to form a powerful, loyal undead legion under their absolute command.

Illusion Magic

  • Phantasmal Foe (Tier 5): Creates a single, semi-real illusion of a creature to distract an opponent. It has no substance but can cause mental distress.
  • Charm Person (Tier 10): Temporarily influences the target to view the caster as a trusted friend and ally. More powerful minds can resist this effect.
  • Maze of the Mind (Tier 15): A powerful illusion that traps a target's consciousness within a mental labyrinth constructed from their own fears and memories.
  • Maximize Magic: Mind Control (Tier 20): An ancient spell that allows for completely dominating another's will

Fire Magic

  • Ignite (Tier 1): A simple utility spell that creates a small, controlled flame to light a torch or campfire
  • Firebolt (Tier 5): The standard offensive fire spell for apprentices, launching a fast-moving bolt of fire at a target
  • Wall of Flame (Tier 10): Conjures a stationary curtain of fire, useful for blocking passages and deterring enemies

Wind Magic

  • Gust (Tier 1): A basic cantrip that produces a targeted puff of air, strong enough to snuff a candle or rustle papers
  • Feather Step (Tier 5): Reduces the caster's weight and muffles their footsteps, allowing for greater agility and stealth
  • Silent Walker (Tier 5): Masks the sounds of the caster's movements, allowing for silent and undetected movement
  • Cyclone Burst (Tier 10): Creates a small, violent vortex of wind to throw enemies off balance and deflect non-magical projectiles

Lightning Magic (Advanced Wind and Fire Magic)

  • Static Charge (Tier 1): A simple utility spell to build up a minor static charge on a metallic object
  • Jolt (Tier 5): Delivers a short-range burst of electricity to stun a target, briefly interrupting their actions
  • Lightning Chains (Tier 15): Shoots out chains of lightning that can paralyze or even kill a weak target instantly
  • Ride the Lightning (Tier 15): A dangerous movement spell that transforms the caster into a bolt of lightning, allowing them to travel near-instantaneously to a visible location.
  • Maximize Magic: Firestorm (Tier 20): An ancient spell that engulfs a vast area in a raging inferno of magical flames

Earth Magic

  • Stone Shape (Tier 5): A utility spell that allows the caster to mold and shape a section of existing stone
  • Earthen Grasp (Tier 10): Causes hands made of solid rock to erupt from the ground and attempt to immobilize a target
  • Tremor (Tier 10): Sends a powerful shockwave through the ground, capable of knocking opponents off their feet and cracking stone structures in a wide area.

Metal Magic (Advanced Earth Magic)

  • Magnetize (Tier 5): An introductory spell allowing the caster to temporarily magnetize a small, non-enchanted metallic object. Often used in workshops or as a trick to pull an opponent's weapon slightly off-balance.
  • Blade Ward (Tier 10): Summons several metallic shards from the caster's mana to orbit them at high speed, automatically intercepting incoming physical projectiles. A signature spell of Dwarven battle-smiths.
  • Shrapnel Burst (Tier 10): Causes a targeted metallic object to explode, sending razor-sharp shards in all directions
  • Summon Metal Golem (Tier 15): Gathers ambient metal and shapes it into a large, powerful, semi-sentient golem that obeys the caster's commands. The golem's strength depends on the quality and quantity of the metal used.
  • Maximize Magic: Adamantine Prison (Tier 20): An ancient Dwarven spell that raises an inescapable prison of magical, unbreakable adamantine around a target, said to be immune to all known forms of damage.

Water/Ice Magic

  • Create Water (Tier 1): A fundamental utility spell used to conjure a small amount of clean, drinkable water
  • Ice Shard (Tier 5): Launches a sharp, fast-moving shard of magical ice at a target
  • Flash Freeze (Tier 10): Instantly freezes a small body of water or rapidly chills a target, encasing them in a layer of frost that hinders movement.
  • Maximize Magic: Absolute Zero (Tier 20): An advanced spell that lowers the temperature of a target to absolute zero, causing instant freezing and potential destruction at a molecular level.

Gravity Magic

  • Zero Gravity (Tier 5): Negates gravity around the target, causing them to float weightlessly
  • Featherfall (Tier 10): Grants the target a gentle descent, negating fall damage
  • Anchor (Tier 10): Increases the target's personal gravity, making them slow, heavy, and difficult to move
  • Gravity Cannon (Tier 15): Creates small areas of collapsing gravity that can cause damage or hinder movement
  • Sanction Grounds (Tier 15): Affects an area that immobilizes targets within it
  • Unlimited Pressure (Tier 20): Generates an immense and unrelenting gravitational force on a specific area
  • Singularity Prison (Super-Tier Spell): A spell that manipulates gravity to a truly absolute degree. The caster targets a single point in space and amplifies its gravitational pull to near-infinite levels, creating a temporary, microscopic singularity—a black hole. The target is not crushed in the conventional sense but is instead trapped within a pocket of warped spacetime from which nothing, not even light, can escape. The consequences are catastrophic; maintaining the singularity requires immense concentration, and its collapse releases a devastating gravitational wave, capable of leveling entire cities.

Space Magic

  • Blink (Tier 10): A short-range, line-of-sight teleportation
  • Space Crack (Tier 15): Connects two different locations with each other by compressing space
  • Maximize Magic; Absorb Elements (Tier 20): The exact effects of this spell are unknown
  • Maximize Magic: Teleportation (Tier 20): Allows for instantaneous teleportation across vast distances
  • Maximize Magic: Warp (Tier 20): This powerful spell allows for instantaneous teleportation to a chosen location
  • Manifold Detachment (Super-Tier Spell): A spell that fundamentally breaks the laws of physics and dimensions. The caster targets a large area—a fortress, a forest, an entire island—and surgically "cuts" it out of the fabric of the plane. The detached section of reality is then shunted into its own isolated, inescapable pocket dimension. This is not teleportation, but a permanent exile from the known universe. The borders of this cosmic wound often become unstable "spatial rifts", leaking the horrors of the void into the world.

Creation Magic

  • Create Homunculus (Tier 10): By manipulating raw mana particles, the caster forms a small, temporary, semi-sentient servant made of magical energy and a chosen element. It can perform simple tasks but dissolves after a short time.
  • Genesis Forge (Tier 15): Allows the user to shape raw matter and mana into a permanent, non-magical object of exquisite complexity, such as a perfectly balanced sword or an intricate key. This spell is the foundation of high-level artifact creation.
  • Maximize Magic: Animate Life (Tier 20): The pinnacle of Creation Magic. By following a complex ritual and expending immense power, the caster can create a truly new, non-magical living being from nothingness. The moral implications of this spell are heavily debated by the Heaven Sect.
  • World Seed Genesis (Super-Tier Spell): The pinnacle of the creation magic, this spell goes beyond creating a single being. The caster imbues a piece of their own soul and an immense amount of mana into a catalyst, creating a World Seed. When placed on a barren or lifeless plane, this seed blossoms into a new, self-sustaining ecosystem, complete with unique magical flora and fauna. This act is considered a Miracle, but it permanently diminishes the caster's own soul, and the new life that evolves is entirely beyond their control, with the potential to become monstrous.

Void Magic

  • Void Lash (Tier 10): Creates a whip-like tendril of pure void energy that erases whatever it touches
  • Void Eyes (Tier 15): The ability to see mana particles, see in the dark and detect invisible creatures and objects
  • Maximize Magic: Void Sphere (Tier 20): Within a designated area, use the void to push out all other elements
  • Event Horizon Annulment (Super-Tier Spell): The ultimate expression of Void Magic's concept of erasure. The caster designates a specific target—be it a living being, an object, or even a magical effect—and completely annuls its existence from a brief period of causality. The target doesn't just die or break; it is removed from the timeline as if it were never there, forcing reality to violently stitch itself together around the new paradox. The consequence of such a flagrant violation of reality is immense; the target may suffer memory loss or have parts of their own history erased, and the area can be plagued by unstable spatial phenomena for centuries.

School of Thaumaturgy

  • Boomerang Gale (Tier 1): Creates razor-edged wind blades that autonomously attack the target
  • Flame Tongue (Tier 1): A small, localized explosion that deals minor damage for a low mana cost
  • Sun Flame (Tier 10): Engulfs the target in flames hot enough to melt even the sturdiest metals
  • Water Wyrm (Tier 5): Shapes a designated body of water into a powerful serpent that attacks the target
  • Stone Graveyard (Tier 10): Summons massive earthen spikes from the ground to impale enemies
  • Morana's Wail (Tier 5): Launches multiple large icicles at the target

School of Alchemy

Combat Alchemy

  • Everblaze (Tier 10): Creates continuous burns on the target
  • Clay Cannon (Tier 5): Rapidly launches projectiles made of solidified alchemic clay
  • Poison Kiss (Tier 5): Creates a cloud of toxic mist that inflicts progressive lesions
  • Rust Missile (Tier 5): A projectile that corrodes and melts metal on contact
  • Widow's Stream (Tier 15): Shoots out a stream of highly toxic venom that can melt through most organic materials

Synthesis

  • Rust Elixir: A concoction that can corrode metal over time
  • Draught of Clarity: A potion that cures mental ailments and curses
  • Draught of Quick Recovery: A fast-acting potion that restores health and stamina
  • Palmerra: A powerful but dangerous mind-controlling drug
  • Cure-All: A potion that can cure a wide range of ailments and poisons

School of Enchantment (Glyphs)

  • Glyph of Binding: Counters beings that can enter a state of incorporeality
  • Glyph of Runefire: Empowers physical fighters by channeling explosive flames into their weapons
  • Glyph of Subjugation: Takes control of the target's body
  • Glyph of Immunity: Strengthens resistance to specific types of magical effects
  • Glyph of Invisibility: Bends light around the target, rendering them invisible
  • Glyph of Magesong: Transfers a portion of the caster's mana to the target
  • Glyph of Ailment: Induces unpleasant bodily ailments like nausea or dizziness
  • Glyph of Protection: Fortifies the target with a magical shield
  • Glyph of Strength: Augments the target's physical capabilities
  • Glyph of Dispel: A counter-enchantment used to dispel other magical effects
  • Glyph of Terror: Instills overwhelming fear in the target
  • Glyph of Slumber: Induces a state of sleep in the target
  • Glyph of Weakness: Drains the target's energy, leaving them sluggish
  • Glyph of Manaburn: Channels the caster's mana to amplify another's spell
