Appearance

Wesker's true face is an enigma, forever hidden behind a smiling, demonic mask of white porcelain and black markings. This mask is the focal point of his chilling presence, its painted grin never faltering, making his true intentions utterly impossible to read. He is tall and gaunt, clad in layers of dark, ceremonial robes that obscure his form. He moves with a silent, predatory grace, his every gesture deliberate and unnerving.

Personality

A high-ranking lord in the armies of Hell and the Demon Queen's most trusted subordinate, Wesker is the embodiment of cold, calculated cruelty. He is a master of psychological warfare, delighting in the slow, methodical dismantling of his enemies' minds and loyalties. He speaks in a calm, measured tone that is often more terrifying than any shout, and he uses praise and intellectual curiosity as weapons. While he serves the Demon Queen with absolute loyalty, it is a loyalty born of a shared philosophy: that the universe is a game of control, and the greatest victory is not just to defeat an opponent, but to make them break themselves.

Items

The Mask of Many Faces
Wesker's signature mask. It is a divine relic that not only hides his presence from magical detection but allows him to perfectly mimic the voice and mannerisms of any being he has observed.
The Soul-Stitched Scroll
A scroll made from the flayed souls of failed strategists. It contains countless forbidden enchantments and curses, and he can unleash them by simply reading the victim's name from its surface.

Innate Skill

Masquerade of Dread [Mythical, Active]

Wesker's power is to give a face to his opponent's deepest fears. By peering into a target's mind, he can project a perfect, hyper-realistic illusion of the person or entity they dread most.

This illusion is not merely visual; it speaks with the victim's own voice of self-doubt, knows their most secret failures, and can even appear to wield their own abilities against them. It is the ultimate tool for turning an enemy's mind into their own personal prison.

Arts & Spells

Life Thread Severance [Rare, Forbidden Art]

A forbidden assassination art. The user acquires a piece of the target's biological matter and performs a complex ritual to perceive and sever their "life thread" in the Ethereal Plane, causing death from afar.

Hollow Sphere [Rare, Active]

A rare and highly specialized defensive art developed as a direct counter to a Domain Declaration. It erects a small, personal barrier of "unwritten" reality that nullifies the unavoidable hit effect of any domain it is cast within.

Domain Declaration - The Carnival of Broken Wills [Legendary, Active]

Wesker's domain transforms the battlefield into a twisted, never-ending carnival filled with distorted mirrors and unsettling marionettes that wear the faces of the target's loved ones. The guaranteed-hit effect is a continuous psychic assault; the mirrors reflect the target's worst failures, the marionettes whisper their deepest insecurities, and the cheerful carnival music becomes a maddening cacophony that erodes sanity and willpower, leaving the victim a hollow shell.
