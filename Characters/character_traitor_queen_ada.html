Appearance

Ada retains the otherworldly beauty and regal grace that once won the hearts of her subjects, making her betrayal all the more chilling. She wears elegant, dark gowns that blend royal finery with the subtle, unnerving symbols of the Divine Cult. She moves with a serene confidence, her expression often a placid, unreadable mask. Her eyes, once warm, now hold a cold, pragmatic light that reveals nothing of her true motivations.

Personality

The true enigma of the Divine Cult. Once the beloved queen of a prosperous human kingdom, Ada shocked the world by betraying her people to join the Void Monarch. On the surface, her mind is a fortress; she operates with a tactical brilliance and pragmatism that is utterly detached from conventional morality. It is unknown whether she was coerced, converted, or is playing a deeper game. This ambiguity, combined with her unshakable composure, makes her a dangerous and unpredictable force. She has traded the love of her people for a belief in a harsher order, a philosophy that aligns perfectly with her new master... or so it seems.

System Alert: High-priority target. Mandate is to retrieve unharmed. Lethal force is not authorized. Asset is critical.

Items

Phoenix Rebirth Pendant
A mythical artifact enchanted with the one-time use skill "Death Transfer." In response to immediate mortal peril, the skill activates and transfers all damage from the bearer to the attacker. A gift from the Heavenly Demon, it is a symbol of their enigmatic bond.

A beautiful and ornate scepter that was once the symbol of her rule. It now serves as a potent focus for her magic and a grim reminder of the kingdom she abandoned.

Innate Skill

Sovereign's Will [Mythical, Active]

Ada's unique ability as a born ruler, now perfected as a battlefield commander. She can place a "Sovereign's Mark" on a limited number of targets, friend or foe.

  • Mark of Favor (Ally): Marked allies receive enhanced reflexes, stamina, and a direct, silent telepathic link to Ada, allowing for flawless tactical coordination.
  • Mark of Anathema (Enemy): Marked enemies become beacons on the battlefield. Ada can subtly influence their perception, making them misjudge distances or mistake allies for foes. As a final command, she can force one marked enemy to unleash a single, uncontrollable attack on another marked enemy.

Arts

Aura Suppression [Common, Active]

A mental discipline allowing a sorcerer to consciously dampen their own mana signature, making them appear less powerful or even non-magical.

Mana Imprint [Rare, Active]

An art used to "imprint" a piece of one's unique mana signature onto an object. This creates a sympathetic link, allowing the creator to feel the general location and condition of the item from a great distance.

Domain Declaration - The Gilded Court of Lies [Legendary, Active]

Ada's domain recreates a spectral, twisted version of her former throne room. It is not a place of direct violence, but of absolute psychological warfare. The guaranteed-hit effect is not a physical attack, but a powerful illusion that preys on the target's deepest regrets, fears, and doubts. Allies appear as enemies, cherished memories become sources of torment, and the target's own power seems to turn against them, leaving them mentally broken and vulnerable.

Skills

Grand Strategist [Rare, Passive]

The user's mind operates like a perfect tactical engine. They can perceive weaknesses in enemy formations, predict probable outcomes of any engagement, and direct their armies with flawless efficiency.

Cursed Speech [Rare, Active]

Allows the user to imbue mana into words that affect targets. Words like "Hesitate," "Falter," or "Betray" can have devastating effects on the battlefield when spoken by a master.
