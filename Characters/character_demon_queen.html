Appearance

Edvina presents herself with a chilling and regal beauty, her pale skin a stark contrast to her sharp, black horns and the vast, leathery wings that rest behind her. She is often seen seated upon a throne of jagged obsidian and cooled iron, dressed in elegant but imposing dark attire that seems woven from solidified shadow. Her eyes glow with a cold, calculating intelligence, and her every movement is precise and deliberate, betraying none of her true intentions.

Personality

A formidable leader from the ordered realm of Hell, Edvina stands at the forefront of the Devil race with cunning and strategic brilliance. She is a grand strategist who views the Universe as a cosmic chessboard. Her every action and word is calculated to further her ultimate goal: to control and dominate all of existence. She rules through intellect, fear, and unbreakable infernal contracts, not through brute force. She is patient and regal, possessing an unshakable confidence that comes from millennia of outmaneuvering her enemies.

Items

The Sovereign's Crown
A crown of black iron that is said to contain the True Names of countless enslaved souls, allowing her to draw upon their power.
Scroll of a Thousand Pacts
An ancient, ever-extending scroll upon which all of her infernal contracts are written. It is a divine relic that enforces the terms of her bargains across all planes.
Scepter of Tyranny
A staff topped with a captive, screaming flame from the deepest pits of Hell. It acts as a symbol of her authority and a potent focus for her fire magic.

Arts

Mana Sense [Common, Active]

A fundamental art where a sorcerer actively expands their awareness to feel the flow and concentration of mana in their immediate surroundings.

Kinetic Redirection [Uncommon, Active]

A defensive reflex art where a sorcerer uses a precise, localized burst of force to alter the trajectory of an incoming physical projectile.

Hollow Sphere [Rare, Active]

A rare and highly specialized defensive art developed as a direct counter to a Domain Declaration. It erects a small, personal barrier of "unwritten" reality that nullifies the unavoidable hit effect of any domain it is cast within.

Infernal Decree [Legendary, Active]

Allows the user to issue a single, binding command to any being of a lower rank in their immediate vicinity. Those without sufficient willpower are magically compelled to obey.

Domain Declaration - The Iron Court of Judgment [Legendary, Active]

Edvina's domain transforms the battlefield into a terrifying courtroom from the depths of Hell. Within this court, she is the absolute judge and jury. The guaranteed-hit effect manifests as unbreakable chains of judgment that bind her targets and subject them to the "laws" of her court, which can include the negation of magic, the draining of life force, or forced servitude.

Sovereign Edict [Mythical, Active]

An art learned from the True Law of the World, allows Edvina to impose a single, absolute rule upon a designated area. By declaring a simple, prohibitive command (an 'edict'), she overwrites the natural laws within that space, enforcing her will upon it. For example, she could declare "No flight" or "No healing," and reality within the edict's bounds would bend to make it so.

Skills

Grand Strategist [Rare, Passive]

The user's mind operates like a perfect tactical engine. They can perceive weaknesses in enemy formations, predict probable outcomes of any engagement, and direct their armies with flawless efficiency.

Higher Tiered Physical Nullification [Rare, Active]

A skill that completely nullifies the physical attacks of low-level weapons and monsters below the fourth rank.

Hereditary Skills

Contractual Dominion

Edvina's innate skill as an Archdevil. Anyone who enters into a verbal or written contract with her is magically bound to its terms. She can perceive the thoughts of those bound to her and enforce obedience through excruciating pain with a mere thought.

Binding Word

A form of Cursed Speech unique to devils that allows devils to bind those weaker than themselves to their will. When invoked, it can compel obedience or inflict pain upon the target.

Created Spells

Soul-Pact Brand (Tier 15)
A complex enchantment that places a magical brand on a target. This brand allows Edvina to slowly siphon their power and knowledge over time, and it acts as a permanent tracking beacon across the planes.
