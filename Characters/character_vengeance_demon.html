Appearance

Kaelen presents a figure of immense, unyielding strength. His body is encased in a suit of dark, jagged armor that seems to be a part of him, glowing with faint, violet light from the cracks. He moves with the slow, deliberate certainty of a tectonic plate, his presence exuding not malice, but an overwhelming sense of grim determination and absolute resolve. His face is rarely seen, but his eyes glow from within his helm with a cold, unwavering light.

Personality

As the emperor of a faction perpetually on the defensive, Kaelen is the embodiment of pragmatism. He is not a kind ruler, nor is he needlessly cruel; every decision is weighed on a scale of strategic necessity. He carries the immense burden of protecting humanity from a world of horrors, and this has forged him into a being of pure, unshakeable will. He is respected, not loved, and feared by his enemies not for his aggression, but for his legendary ability to endure any assault and return the pain he receives a hundredfold. His title comes from his core philosophy: true victory is achieved by making the enemy regret they ever chose to attack.

Items

Aegis of Eternity
One of the seven sentient weapons of the Primordial Armory. A large shield whose surface is a shimmering temporal distortion. It offers absolute defense by displacing attacks in time, shunting a physical blow a few seconds into the future or aging a spell until it dissipates.
Last Stand Armor
A suit of enchanted adamantine armor that is magically linked to the user's life force. The closer the wearer is to death, the more resilient and unbreakable the armor becomes.
The Oathkeeper's Blade
A simple, unadorned longsword that cannot be broken. It is said to carry the weight of every oath sworn upon it, and its strikes land with the force of a nation's collective will.

Innate Skill

Retribution's Echo [Mythical, Passive/Active]

Kaelen's signature ability and the source of his title. Passively, his body and soul act as a damage sink; he feels a fraction of the pain and harm dealt to all allies sworn to him within a certain radius. He can then activate the skill to unleash this stored suffering.

When activated, he summons a spectral, perfect reflection of his opponent. This "Echo" is intangible and immune to harm, and for a short duration, it unleashes the entirety of the stored damage back upon its original source in a single, overwhelming counter-attack.

Arts

Iron Fortress Stance [Rare, Active]

A master-level defensive art. The user adopts a rooted stance and circulates a massive amount of mana throughout their body. While in this stance, the user gains immense resilience to physical blows and becomes nearly immovable, at the cost of mobility.

Hollow Sphere [Rare, Active]

A rare defensive art that creates a personal barrier of "unwritten" reality, nullifying the unavoidable hit effect of any Domain Declaration it is cast within.

Domain Declaration - Bastion of Retribution [Legendary, Active]

Kaelen's domain transforms the battlefield into a desolate, cratered wasteland under a stormy sky, littered with the ghostly images of fallen soldiers. Within this domain, his authority is absolute. The guaranteed-hit effect manifests as spectral chains that erupt from the ground, binding his target. For every attack the target makes while chained, a ghostly soldier rises and strikes them with an identical, unavoidable blow, turning their own aggression into a self-destructive barrage.
