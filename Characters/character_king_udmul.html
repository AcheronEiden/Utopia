Appearance

King Udmul is the epitome of a Dwarven king: broad, immensely powerful, and radiating an aura of unshakable stability. His long, silver beard is intricately braided and clasped with runes of power. He is always seen in a suit of masterfully crafted golden armor, so perfectly fitted that it moves with him as a second skin. He sits upon a throne of solid granite, his presence not one of menace, but of the deep, quiet strength of the mountain itself.

Personality

A just and compassionate king from the grand, subterranean halls of the Dwarven Kingdom, King Udmul leads his people with profound wisdom and fairness. He is a bastion of tradition who views his kingdom not as a territory to be held, but as a family to be nurtured. Unlike the sorcerers who weave magic, Udmul's power is direct and tangible, rooted in the discipline of a master martial artist and the unparalleled skill of a runesmith. He is slow to anger but implacable once roused, fighting with the straightforward, overwhelming force of an avalanche.

Items

Aether, The World Shaper
One of the seven sentient weapons of the Primordial Armory. A pair of gauntlets woven from solidified nebula. They grant unparalleled control over elements, particularly Metal and Lightning, allowing the user to transmute matter and raise structures with a gesture.
Heart of the Mountain
A suit of living adamantine armor forged by Udmul himself. It is directly linked to his life force and can repair itself from any damage as long as he draws breath.
World-Breaker
A colossal warhammer forged from the heart of a fallen star. Its strikes land with the force of a meteor impact, and the runes etched upon it can shatter magical barriers and dispel enchantments.

Innate Skill

Soul of the Forge [Mythical, Passive]

Udmul's essence as a master artificer and warrior made manifest. His soul is intrinsically linked to every item he has personally forged. This has two effects:

  • Symbiotic Power: The more of his own creations he wears and wields, the stronger his physical attributes become. His armor is not just protection; it is an extension of his own durability. His hammer is not just a weapon; it is an extension of his own strength.
  • Runic Awakening: By channeling his own stamina and mana directly into an item he has forged, he can temporarily awaken its latent potential, unlocking hidden powers or pushing its enchantments far beyond their normal limits for a devastating final blow.

Techniques

Iron Fist [Common, Active]

The user concentrates a dense amount of mana into their fist, hardening it to the consistency of iron for a single, powerful blow.

Impact Wave [Uncommon, Active]

The user channels a significant amount of mana into a physical strike and releases it in a concussive burst upon impact, creating a short-range shockwave that can stagger nearby enemies.

Iron Fortress Stance [Rare, Active]

A master-level defensive technique. The user adopts a rooted stance and circulates a massive amount of mana throughout their body. While in this stance, the user gains immense resilience to physical blows and becomes nearly immovable.

Heaven-Shaking Stomp [Legendary, Active]

The pinnacle of channeling mana through the body. The user focuses their entire mana reserves into a single, devastating stomp. The resulting shockwave sends a resonance wave through the very ley lines of the earth, creating a localized, violent earthquake that can level structures and shatter armies.
