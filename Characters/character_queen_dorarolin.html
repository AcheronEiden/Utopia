Appearance

Dorarolin cuts a starkly different figure from a traditional Dwarf. While she possesses their inherent sturdiness, she carries herself with the cold, arrogant grace of a sorceress. Her haid is short and meticulously styled, and her eyes hold a sharp, calculating glint. She is always adorned in opulent robes woven with threads of gold and silver, and wears jewelry crafted from cursed, yet beautiful, gemstones that seem to drink the light. Her presence is not one of warmth, but of the cold, unyielding pressure of the deep earth.

Personality

Consumed by a bottomless jealousy and an insatiable thirst for power, Dorarolin is a volatile and cunning ruler who holds sway over a significant portion of the Dwarven people. She sees Udmul's mercy and tradition as weakness, believing that true power lies in absolute control and the acquisition of wealth. Her every action is a calculated move to undermine her rival, amass personal power, and prove that her mastery of sorcery—a path often viewed with suspicion by her people—is superior to the "outdated" ways of the forge and the axe. She is a master of political intrigue, preying on the greed of others to sow dissent within the Dwarven Kingdom.

A twisted crown of gold and cursed gems that whispers temptations into the ears of those around her, amplifying their greed and making them more susceptible to her manipulations.

A scepter carved from a single, flawless diamond. It is a powerful focus for her Metal Magic and has the terrifying ability to transmute living flesh into inert, crystalline statues with a single touch.

Innate Skill

Heartstone Corruption [Mythical, Active]

Dorarolin's ultimate expression of greed and control. She can sense and manipulate the "Heartstone" of any object or being—the metaphysical core that represents its value and integrity.

  • On objects: She can corrupt the core of a masterwork weapon, causing it to shatter on its next impact, or taint a vein of precious ore, turning it to worthless dust.
  • On beings: By focusing on a target, she can corrupt their "Heartstone," twisting their strongest convictions into their greatest weaknesses. A noble warrior's honor becomes crippling self-doubt, a sorcerer's ambition becomes reckless abandon. It is a power that does not break the body, but the soul.

Arts

Domain Declaration - The Tyrant's Treasury [Legendary, Active]

Dorarolin's domain transforms the battlefield into an immense, subterranean vault filled with mountains of shimmering gold and jewels. The air itself feels heavy with avarice. The guaranteed-hit effect manifests as the treasure itself coming to life: waves of liquid gold attempt to drown her enemies, chains of diamonds erupt to bind them, and the very ground can transmute into razor-sharp crystals to impale them.

Backstory

Dorarolin hails from the prestigious Blackvein Clan, known for their unparalleled skill in metallurgy and enchantment. From a young age, she exhibited a prodigious talent for magic, much to the chagrin of her traditionalist family. Her obsession with power and wealth grew as she delved deeper into forbidden magics, ultimately leading her to challenge the established order of the Dwarven Kingdom.

Her rise to power was marked by ruthless ambition and cunning political maneuvers. She orchestrated coups, manipulated trade routes to amass wealth, and formed alliances with unsavory factions, including dark sorcerers and mercenaries. Her ultimate goal is to usurp King Udmul and reshape the Dwarven Kingdom in her own image—a realm where power is absolute, and greed is celebrated.

Dorarolin's relationship with Udmul is one of bitter rivalry. She views his mercy and adherence to tradition as weaknesses that have made the kingdom vulnerable. In contrast, she believes that only through strength, fear, and the accumulation of wealth can true order be maintained. This ideological clash has led to a deep-seated animosity between the two, with Dorarolin constantly seeking ways to undermine his rule and prove her superiority.
