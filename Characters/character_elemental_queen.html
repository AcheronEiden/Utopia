Appearance

Melian appears as a regal elven woman with long, dark hair that carries a deep emerald sheen, and her eyes glow with the soft light of nature itself. She adorns herself in elegant robes woven from moonlight and leaves, accented with living wood and glowing emeralds. Her presence does not exude fear, but rather the immense, ancient pressure of a deep forest or the calm power of a gathering storm.

Personality

Melian is the embodiment of balance and preservation. She is deeply empathetic to the natural world, able to feel the pain of a blighted forest or the joy of a new bloom across her entire domain. Her wisdom is born from millennia of patience, viewing time through the slow growth of ancient trees. While she is serene and gentle by nature, her disposition changes dramatically when the balance of her domain is threatened. In such times, her calm demeanor gives way to the ferocity of a hurricane or the unyielding power of an earthquake, proving she is a fierce protector of her people and the natural world.

Hereditary Skills

One with the World

A passive skill reflecting her deep connection to nature. Within her domain, she can see through the eyes of any animal and hear the whispers carried on the wind, making it impossible to surprise her.

Nature's Retribution

The very forest she protects acts as an extension of her will. When Melian is attacked, the ancient trees, vines, and even the ground itself will move to ensnare, crush, or strike down her enemies without a direct command.

Whispers of the Forest

Melian can communicate with all souls within her domain, allowing her to project tranquility to calm armies or project her will to command roots and vines to crush her targets.

Innate Skill

Elemental Dominion [Mythical, Passive/Active]

This is the source of the Elemental Queen's legendary resilience and the ultimate defense of her domain. Her life force is not merely her own; it is intrinsically tethered to the entirety of the Whispering Woods. Her physical body is but a vessel, a focal point for her consciousness.

When faced with a threat she cannot otherwise overcome or an attack that would prove fatal, Melian can activate the skill's true nature. She voluntarily unravels her physical form, dispersing her consciousness and spiritual essence into the very fabric of the forest—the ancient trees, the flowing streams, the living soil, and the air itself. In this state, she is everywhere and nowhere, rendering her immune to conventional harm.

As long as a single leaf remains in her domain, she cannot be truly killed. She can reconstitute her physical body anywhere within the woods, fully restored. This turns any assassination attempt into a grueling war of attrition against an entire, living world—a war an invader is guaranteed to lose.
