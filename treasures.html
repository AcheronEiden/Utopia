Unordinary Treasures

6.1 The Black Orb

A divine relic renowned for unparalleled durability, it possesses the remarkable ability to metamorphose into any weapon envisioned by its wielder. What instills fear is that upon transformation, the user seamlessly gains proficiency in wielding the chosen weapon, accompanied by the acquisition of battle expertise equivalent to several years of experience. This weapon is crafted from the scales of an eighth-rank Void Dragon, and further enhanced by being bathed in the fiery essence of a dying Infernal Phoenix. It is currently in the hands of the Dwarven Race.

6.2 Phoenix Rebirth Pendant

A mythical artefact enchanted with the one-time use ancient skill Death Transfer. It is said that in response to immediate mortal peril to the wearer, the skill is activated and takes all the damage away from its bearer, transferring it to the attacker. The pendant is currently with the Traitor Queen Ada. Given to her by the Heavenly Demon, a symbol of their enigmatic bond.

6.3 The Primordial Armory

In the eons before recorded history, the Primordial Gods waged wars that shaped the cosmos. The weapons they wielded were not mere tools but extensions of their will, imbued with such immense power that they developed their own sentience over millennia. Legends whisper of seven such artifacts scattered across the planes, each waiting for a wielder worthy of its unimaginable power.

1. Balerion (Sword) - The Black Dread

A cursed black sword that becomes part of its wielder's body and lives within them. It can be stored as a tattoo when not in use. Balerion is an extremely durable and sharp sword capable of cutting through anything. Balerion continuously drains aura from its user's body, which could potentially exhaust the wielder. The Blood Form is its strongest manifestation, unlocked by a Fifth Order Being, which absorbs the user's blood and aura to grant a huge jump in power. The weapon is in the possession of the Moth Man Lucifer (most likely given to the Demonic Army).

2. Dawn Guard (Staff) - The Light Bringer

A staff carved from the petrified heartwood of a star-fallen tree with a perpetually warm, glowing crystal at its cap. Its sentience is noble and discerning. It is the ultimate conduit for Light and Holy Magic, passively purifying the wielder's mana and capable of unleashing beams of concentrated light. At its peak, it is said to grant temporary insight into the True Law of Immortality. The staff is currently held by the Elemental Queen Melian.

3. Aether (Gloves) - The World Shaper

A pair of gauntlets woven from solidified nebula, with faint constellations swirling across their surface. The Aether's sentience is that of a master artisan. The gloves grant unparalleled control over elements, particularly Metal and Lightning, allowing the user to transmute matter and raise structures with a gesture. They are a definitive tool for Creation Magic. Currently in the possession of King Udmul the Merciful.

4. Aegis of Eternity (Shield) - The Timeless Bulwark

A large, circular shield whose surface is a shimmering temporal distortion, a window into moments past and future. The Aegis offers absolute defense by displacing attacks in time. A physical blow might be shunted a few seconds into the future, and a powerful spell can be absorbed and released later or aged until it dissipates. Currently wielded by the The Vengeance Demon.

5. Whisper of Oblivion (Dagger) - The Void's Tooth

A cruel-looking dagger whose blade seems to be a tear in the fabric of reality itself. Its sentience is cold and predatory. A wound from this dagger does not bleed blood; it bleeds existence, unraveling mana and life force and preventing magical healing. It allows the wielder to take short, untraceable steps through the void. Current whereabouts unknown.

6. Starfall (Bow) - The Comet's Flight

A longbow crafted from the silver light of a dead constellation, with a bowstring of captured gravity. Starfall requires no physical arrows; instead, it materializes arrows of condensed starlight. Its arrows are said to travel with the unstoppable force of a falling star. Current whereabouts unknown.

7. The Weaver's Lyre (Instrument) - The Symphony of Fate

A masterfully crafted lyre with strings of pure, solidified mana. Its sentience is artistic and capricious. Its power lies in manipulating reality through melodies. A soothing tune can influence the True Law of Emotions, while a dissonant chord can twist the True Law of Perceived Reality, trapping foes in illusions. Currently held by the Blood Empire.
