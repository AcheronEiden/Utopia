The Utopia World Manual

A Guide for the Martial Artist

This compendium details the Skills known to the Martial Artists of the world. Unlike a Sorcerer's spells, which are woven from imagination, these Skills are born from discipline, repetition, and the channeling of mana through the physical form.

Common Skills

Reinforce
General Use

The most fundamental technique for any warrior. The user channels mana into a part of their body to temporarily harden their skin and muscles. A reinforced arm can block a blade, and a reinforced leg can withstand a heavy impact. It is the first step in learning to treat the body as a weapon.

Flowing Step
General Use

A basic footwork technique where the user channels a small, continuous stream of mana to their feet. This lightens their steps and allows them to move more smoothly and quickly across the battlefield, enabling rapid dodges and repositioning.

Piercing Thrust
Spearman

The first skill any aspiring spearman learns. The user channels a steady stream of mana to the very tip of their spear, hardening it and creating a focused point of force. This allows the thrust to punch through mundane armor that would otherwise deflect the blow.

Quick Draw
Swordsman

By channeling a burst of mana to their arm and hand, the user can draw their blade from its sheath at incredible speed for a surprise attack or a swift defense.

Steady Aim
Bowman

The user channels mana into their eyes, hands, and arms to steady their shot, dramatically increasing accuracy over long distances.

Active Guard
Shield User

The user channels mana into their shield at the moment of blocking. This reinforces the shield's structure to absorb more kinetic force, reducing stamina loss and preventing the user from being knocked back by heavy blows.

Uncommon Skills

Ricochet Shot
Bowman

The user fires an arrow infused with a specific mana frequency, allowing it to bounce off one physical surface without losing its momentum or lethality.

Shield Bash
Shield User

Focusing a burst of mana into their shield, the user executes a bash that not only deals heavy concussive damage but also temporarily disorients the target.

Second Wind
General Use

By performing a specialized breathing pattern, the user circulates mana through their body to forcefully expel physical exhaustion and lactic acid from their muscles. This provides a quick, potent burst of renewed stamina in the midst of a prolonged battle.

Threat Sense
General Use

The user extends a thin, almost imperceptible veil of their mana around their body. This aura allows them to feel incoming hostile intent or fast-moving objects in their blind spots, granting them a slight precognitive edge in combat.

Sweeping Gale
Spearman

The user channels mana along the entire length of their spear and performs a wide, horizontal sweep. The mana is released in a sharp arc, creating a crescent-shaped wave of force capable of knocking multiple unarmored opponents off their feet.

Iai Strike
Swordsman

A skill focused entirely on the speed of a draw-cut. The user channels a massive burst of mana into their arm in the single instant they draw their sword, resulting in a strike that is deceptively fast and incredibly powerful.

Rare Skills

Grand Strategist
General Use

The user's mind operates like a perfect tactical engine. They can perceive weaknesses in enemy formations, predict probable outcomes of any engagement, and direct their armies with flawless efficiency.

Seeking Arrow
Bowman

After firing, the user maintains intense focus, allowing them to subtly manipulate the arrow's trajectory mid-flight with their mana to track a moving target.

Phantom Blade
Swordsman

The swordsman infuses their blade with mana that mimics light and shadow, causing the weapon to flicker and become partially transparent during movement. This optical illusion makes it incredibly difficult for an opponent to track the blade's true position, creating openings in their defense.

Sunken Pike
Spearman

A deceptive technique where the spearman thrusts their spear into the ground, channeling a large amount of mana into it. The spear then travels through the earth as if it were water, re-emerging at high speed from a different location to impale the target from an unexpected angle.

Aegis Reflection
Shield User

A master-class counter-offensive skill where the user absorbs the kinetic energy of a powerful physical blow into their mana-infused shield. The user can then immediately release that stored energy in a devastating, forward-facing concussive blast.

Higher Tiered Physical Nullification
General Use

A skill that completely nullifies the physical attacks of low-level weapons and monsters below the fourth rank

Cursed Speech
General Use

Allows the user to imbue mana into words that affect targets. The more mana used, the more powerful words can be used but with higher risk and drawbacks to the user.
